This is Hello World

Hello World essentially is a web page which enables people to just look at this website without a care in the world. By looking at this webpage, it will not do you any benefit or gain any valuable message. It is just another plain old website which someone made during the Vesak National Holiday. There will be images of the beautiful world later in the section with the header name "This is The Universe". Although the header name "This is The Universe" does not really corelate with its' body, it will give the reader of this webpage a fresh reminder of how much time has been wasted by visiting this webpage.

This is The Universe

This is to show an image of what this world really is, however this is only a little depiction of this awesome world. It is definitely not a portrait of the universe, the universe is the existence of everything. The air, water, soil, humans, animals, pretty much every living thing and dead thing combined is the universe. The universe itself is limitless, however we can say hello to the universe, "Hello Universe", there i said hello to something that is limitless. The one way conversation is sometimes a metaphor to say "world please be good today" or "hello everything, i once again meet you after a long night sleep, we get to face eachother once more, bring it on". However i say it just to make conversation with the world, i am very much lonely in this quarantine hours, but probably that will be terifying should the universe greet me back.
